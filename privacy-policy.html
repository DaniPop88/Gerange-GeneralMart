Privacy Policy

At Gerame Polangcos General Mart, protecting your personal information is one of our highest priorities. This policy outlines the ways in which we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our store or website.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as your name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Order and transaction information
  • Any feedback, inquiries, or requests you submit to us

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders efficiently
  • To communicate with you about your purchases, promotions, or updates
  • To improve our products, services, and customer experience
  • To comply with legal requirements and safeguard our business

How We Protect Your Data

We use secure systems and procedures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of your personal information. Only authorized team members have access to your data, and we never sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You may review, update, or request removal of your personal information by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies to improve performance and personalize your experience. We may also collect anonymous analytics data to help us understand how our site is used. You can adjust your browser settings to manage or block cookies.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
