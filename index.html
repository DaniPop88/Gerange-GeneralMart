About Us

Gerame Polangcos General Mart was established in 2021 with a simple purpose: to provide affordable and quality clothing for hardworking Filipino families. Located in the heart of Marikina City—the Shoe Capital of the Philippines—we are proud to be part of the local community, offering carefully selected products that combine practicality and style.

Our founder, Gerame Polangcos, is a true-blue Marikeño who built this mart upon the values of honesty, affordability, and “malasakit sa customer”. We believe that shopping should not break the bank, and every customer deserves excellent service and good finds.

Our Mission

To make stylish, everyday apparel accessible to all Filipinos—mula bata hanggang lolo’t lola.

Our Vision

To become Marikina’s most trusted and recommended general clothing mart by delivering consistent quality and value.

Products & Services

We offer a curated range of apparel and essentials tailored for the Filipino lifestyle. You’ll find both local brands and select imported items, with new stocks arriving weekly!

  • 👕 Men’s Apparel: Polo shirts, casual tees, jackets, office wear & barongs, budget-friendly basics.
  • 👗 Women’s Apparel: Dresses, blouses, pants, homewear & loungewear, work-ready outfits.
  • 🧒 Kids’ Clothing: School wear, pambahay, uniforms, trendy & comfy everyday options.
  • 🧢 Accessories: Hats, belts, socks, bags, local crafts & seasonal items.
  • 🧺 Household Essentials: Towels, bedsheets, blankets, affordable daily-use items.

All products are handpicked to offer both affordability and style. New arrivals every week!
